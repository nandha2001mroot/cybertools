About MAC Addresses

A MAC (Media Access Control) address is a unique identifier assigned to network interfaces for communications at the physical layer of a network. It consists of 6 bytes (48 bits) and is commonly expressed in hexadecimal format.

Common Formats:
  • Colon Format: 00:1A:2B:3C:4D:5E
  • Hyphen Format: 00-1A-2B-3C-4D-5E
  • Dotted Format: 001A.2B3C.4D5E
  • Plain Format: 001a2b3c4d5e
