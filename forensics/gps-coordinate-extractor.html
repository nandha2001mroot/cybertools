About GPS Metadata

GPS metadata in images contains geolocation information embedded by devices with GPS capabilities. This information is valuable for digital forensics and investigative work.

Common GPS Tags:
  • GPSLatitude: Decimal degrees latitude
  • GPSLongitude: Decimal degrees longitude
  • GPSAltitude: Altitude in meters
  • GPSTimeStamp: UTC time of GPS fix
  • GPSImgDirection: Direction of image
