About Steganography

Steganography is the practice of concealing messages or information within other non-secret text or data. It involves embedding data in ways that avoid detection.

Common Steganography Techniques:
  • LSB (Least Significant Bit): Embedding data in the least significant bits of pixels
  • DCT (Discrete Cosine Transform): Modifying frequency coefficients in JPEG
  • Spread Spectrum: Distributing data across the spectrum
  • Echo Hiding: Embedding in audio waveforms
