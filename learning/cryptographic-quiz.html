About Cryptographic Concepts

Cryptography is the practice and study of techniques for secure communication in the presence of third parties called adversaries. It plays a vital role in protecting data confidentiality, integrity, and authenticity.

Key Cryptographic Concepts:
  • Symmetric Encryption: Uses the same key for encryption and decryption
  • Asymmetric Encryption: Uses a pair of keys (public and private)
  • Hash Functions: Create fixed-size digests of variable-length input
  • Digital Signatures: Provide authentication and non-repudiation
  • Key Exchange: Securely establish shared keys
