About ROT13

ROT13 ("rotate by 13 places") is a simple letter substitution cipher that replaces a letter with the letter 13 letters after it in the alphabet. It is a special case of the Caesar cipher.

Characteristics:
  • ROT13 is its own inverse
  • Applies only to letters
  • Numbers and symbols remain unchanged
  • Used for obfuscation, not security
Expert Insight
GraphQL Security

In GraphQL introspection testing, ROT13 might be used for simple obfuscation of query parameters or sensitive information that should be easily reversible.
