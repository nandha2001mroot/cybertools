About Port Scanning

Port scanning is the process of probing a server or host for open ports. Common port ranges include:

  • Well-known ports (0-1023): System services like SSH, HTTP, HTTPS
  • Registered ports (1024-49151): User applications
  • Dynamic ports (49152-65535): Temporary connections
Legal Note: Only scan networks you own or have explicit permission to test. Unauthorized port scanning may be illegal.
